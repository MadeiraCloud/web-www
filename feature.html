Design Your Vision in Stack

  • Drag-n-drop to Build

    Building a cloud has never been easier than with VisualOps. With its "WYSIWYG" approach, just focus on the architecture design: No new DSL, No new syntax.

  • Top-down DevOps

    Instead of the "bottom-up" workflow of the tranditional DevOps tools, VisualOps takes the top-down approach: build the overall architecture first, then drill down to the instances. It matches better with how our brains works.

  • One Click to Launch

    Grab some coffee! We take care of all heave-lift: provisiong the resources, configure the instances, deployment the code, etc. When you're back, the app will be ready to use.

  • Export

    You can export your design to png files for demoing the system.

    You can export your design to CloudFormation scripts.

    Also, you can export your design json files, so your colleage can import them to continue the work; or simply for version control!

  • Cost Estimation

    VisualOps gives you the realtime cost estimation when you design the app, with the detailed bill broken down to each service.

App State Always Ensured

  • App Ensure

    Traffic overload? Bug in the new version? Server crash? VisualOps gets your apps covered. By doing automation, VisualOps ensures your app always runs in the state you defined.

  • Lifecycle Managemment

    launch/update/start/stop/reboot/terminate
