Drag-n-Drop to build

Building a cloud has never been easier than MadeiraCloud. The seamless drag-n-drop experience isolates all noise and lets you focus on the infrastructure design.

Instead of the "bottom-up" workflow of the tranditional DevOps tools, MadeiraCloud lets you work from top-down: design the overall architecture, then drill down to the instances. It matches better with how our brains works.
