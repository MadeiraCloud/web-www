Focus on the application itself

  • Drag-n-drop to Build

    Building the infrastrucuture has never been easier than with VisualOps. With its "WYSIWYG" approach, just focus on the architecture design: No new DSL, No new syntax.

  • DevOps made easy

    Built-in modules to configure the instances, e.g. package, file, git, service, etc,

    Service discovery is a real breeze: i.e. @{db_instance.PrivateIpAddress}

  • One Click to Launch

    Grab some coffee! We take care of all the heavy-lifting: provisiong the resources, configuring the instances, deploying the code, etc. When you're back, the app will be ready to use.

  • Export

    You can export your design to png files for demoing the system.

    You can export your design to CloudFormation scripts.

    Also, you can export your design json files, for collaborative deployment or version control, for example.

  • Cost Estimation

    VisualOps gives you the realtime cost estimation when you design the app, with the detailed bill broken down to each service.

Ensure the app always runs in the desired state

  • App Ensure

    VisualOps automates the configuration process to ensure your app always runs in the defined state, even if:

    • AutoScaling is triggered
    • Any instance is rebooted
    • New code version is deployed
    • Service has crashed

  • Lifecycle Management

    Complete lifecylce features are supported to let you manage the applications:

    launch/update/start/stop/reboot/terminate

  • Change Validation

    People make mistakes, but VisualOps validates any changes before applying. No more surprise!
