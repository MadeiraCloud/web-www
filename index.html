HOW IT WORKS

  • Design

    Drag and drop real AWS resources to a canvas to quickly design your application architecture.

  • Configure

    Dig into detailed configurations of resources and security group rules. No need to remember commands to configure auto scaling group or other resources.

  • Launch

    Launch your design into real enviornment. AWS resources will be created according to your design and configuration with a single click.

VISION

Now, imagine that you can compose the actual systems similar to the way you draw the conceptual diagrams, drag & drop icons and connect them. While you focus on the architecture, the rest of the “heavy-lifting” work is left to us.
