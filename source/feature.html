FEATURES
Creation

  • Drag-n-drop to Build

    Building the infrastructure has never been easier than with VisualOps. With its "WYSIWYG" approach, just focus on the architecture design.No new DSL, and no new syntax.

  • DevOps made easy

    Built-in modules to configure the instances, e.g. package, file, git, service, etc.

    Service discovery is a real breeze: i.e. @{db_instance.PrivateIpAddress}

  • One Click to Launch

    We take care of all the heavy-lifting: provisioning the resources, configuring the instances and deploying the code. Give yourself a break; by the time you're back, the app will be ready to use.

  • Export

    You can export your design to png files for demoing the system.

    You can export your design to CloudFormation scripts.

    You can export your design json files, for collaborative deployment or version control.

  • Cost Estimation

    VisualOps provides you with real-time cost estimation when you design the app, with a detailed bill broken down to each service.

Management

  • App Ensure

    VisualOps automates the configuration process to ensure your app always runs in the defined state, EVEN IF:

    • AutoScaling is triggered
    • Any instance is rebooted
    • New code version is deployed
    • Service has crashed

  • Lifecycle Management

    Complete lifecycle features are supported to let you manage the application:

    launch/update/start/stop/reboot/terminate

  • Change Validation

    VisualOps validates any change before applying. No nasty surprise!
